About Me

I am a full stack data scientist with experience in working at start-ups. I am interested in working with teams to extract meaningful information from data by applying machine learning algorithms to drive business decisions.

I graduated cum laude with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from St. Lawrence University where I was also a student athlete (Tennis). I briefly worked as a full stack developer where I built the backend and frontend for a fully interactive website.

I obtained my masters degree in data science (MSDS) at the University of San Francisco in 2022. I am currently working at Inuvo as a Data Scientist.
